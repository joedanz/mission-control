## About this manual

|  |  |

This guide comes in two halves. **Part I (Guide)** is the map — what Mission Control is and
how it works, in plain English. **Part II (Reference)** is the manual — the exact commands,
tables, endpoints, and knobs for the people who run, extend, or integrate with the system.

A plain-English companion to `README.md` and `AGENTS.md`; the reference half mirrors
`cli/README.md`, `hooks/README.md`, and `daemon/README.md`.
